What Is AAPC?

AAPC (formerly the American Academy of Professional Coders) is the world's largest training and credentialing organization for the business side of healthcare. Founded in 1988, it now has over 200,000 members worldwide. When you earn your CPC credential, you are earning the most widely recognized professional coding certification in the industry.

You already have an AAPC membership and a 2-attempt CPC exam voucher assigned in your account. This series will help you understand how to use everything available to you.

What Your Membership Includes

Professional Resources

  • CPC exam voucher (2 attempts, valid through Jan 2027)
  • Member ID card and digital credentials
  • Access to the AAPC job board
  • Discounts on AAPC code books, courses, and webinars
  • Free and low-cost CEU opportunities

Networking & Community

  • Access to local AAPC chapters and events
  • Networking with credentialed coders in your area
  • AAPC online community forums
  • CEU tracking through your member dashboard
  • Consumer discounts through AAPC Passport Savings

About Your Exam Voucher

Your voucher gives you two attempts at the CPC exam and is valid through January 2027. You do not need to use both attempts, but having a second attempt available takes the pressure off. Registration is covered in Part 3 of this series. For now, confirm you can see the voucher assigned in your AAPC account.

This Week's Tasks: Log in to aapc.com and explore your dashboard including the Career Center and Savings sections. Locate your voucher and confirm it is assigned and active. Then find and review your Member ID card under your account profile.
