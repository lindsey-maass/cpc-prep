About Your Voucher

  • Your voucher covers 2 attempts at the CPC exam and is valid through January 2027.
  • You do not need to use both attempts. The second is there as a safety net.
  • Your voucher is already assigned in your account. You do not need to purchase anything.
  • Important: The name on your AAPC account must exactly match the government-issued photo ID you will present on exam day. Contact AAPC at least 48 hours before your exam if there is a discrepancy.

Before You Register

Complete both of these steps before scheduling your exam:

Step 1 -- Release your exam results to your instructor. Log in to your AAPC account, click Student, click Details next to the class name, and click Agree and Submit. Instructions and screenshots are in the How to Release Test Results Guide posted in Brightspace.
Step 2 -- Complete the CPC Examination Questionnaire. This is posted in Brightspace and is due by Wednesday of this week. It helps your instructor understand where you are in your preparation and how to best support you.

Registration: Step by Step

Refer to the How to Schedule AAPC Exam guide in Brightspace for screenshots of each step.

StepAction
1Log in to your AAPC account at aapc.com
2Click on Purchases in your account menu
3Under Vouchers, click REDEEM next to your CPC exam voucher
4Log in to the Meazure Learning system using credentials from your Notice to Schedule email
5If first time logging in, read and agree to the Privacy Policy
6Verify your profile details. Confirm your name matches your photo ID exactly. Click Continue.
7Read the Examination Admission Requirements, check the I attest box, click Continue
8Click Schedule next to the Certified Professional Coder Examination
9Select your testing format: Live Remote Proctoring or Testing Center
10Select your preferred date and time, confirm your appointment, and save your confirmation email

After You Register

  • Save your confirmation email. You will need it on exam day.
  • For testing center exams, print all confirmation pages and bring them as your admission ticket.
  • For live remote exams, log in 15 to 30 minutes early on exam day to complete check-in.
  • If you need to reschedule, do so at least 48 hours before your appointment to avoid forfeiting your attempt.
  • You are cleared to register whenever you feel ready. Your voucher is valid through January 2027, so there is no rush -- but having a date on the calendar can be a great motivator.
